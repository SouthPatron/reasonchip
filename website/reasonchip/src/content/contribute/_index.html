Goal

For the first time in history, general intelligence and adaptive reasoning have become a commodity which can be automated at scale. Our goal is to build the open source tools for everyone to use within their environment for AI deployment, integration, and automation without them needing to develop their own AI architecture. Just as people have a choice of open source databases for their infrastructure, we want to provide the same for reasoning. We want to be one of the options.
