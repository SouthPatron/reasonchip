Want to add chips?

Yes! Absolutely! The more, the better.

You actually have two choices:

  • You can provide a PyPI package which people can just install and use of your own accord;
  • You can submit your chip for inclusion into the reasonchip package and that means that:
    • Your chip will be available to anyone who installed reasonchip, obviously;
    • Your chip documentation will be available on the official website with the increased visibility and exposure.
    • In the case of issues, I'll bounce them back to you to sort out.

In order of prefence of submitters:

  • Official software integrations from the target software. ie: "VendorChip" from original "Vendor Inc."
  • Definitely not least, anyone else who provides a good solution.
